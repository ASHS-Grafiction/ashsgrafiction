Title: Distance Doesn't Matter
By: kilo525/Kaede Bicomong (artist/co-writer), Average 141/Gerardo Galicia (writer)
Comic Description:
A short story of two friends whose plans of bonding over the summer break are suddenly cut short by circumstances out of their control. Will this unexpected change of circumstance put a stop to all their merryment? Or through different methods, will they continue in their maximixation of the days they have to spend with each other?
